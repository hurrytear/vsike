Why It Exists

vsike started as a question: what would a digital space look like if it was built for the work itself, not for the algorithm that indexes it?

Most portfolios are optimised for first impressions — big hero sections, motion-heavy carousels, personality-free brutalism. vsike is optimised for the second and third visit. The reader who came back because the thinking was worth returning to.

The no-framework constraint is a design decision, not a technical flex. Every component on this site is something a person can read, understand, and modify without a build toolchain. That's a value statement about craft: understand your tools, own your stack, don't abstract away the interesting parts.

The trilingual structure reflects the audience vsike actually speaks to: teams in East Asia building at the edge of AI and design, and the wider global community watching the same frontiers from different vantage points.
